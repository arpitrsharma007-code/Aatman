4. Data Storage & Security

Your account data is stored in MongoDB Atlas (cloud database). Conversation data is transmitted to Anthropic's servers (United States) for AI processing. We implement the following security measures:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest
  • Access controls with role-based permissions
  • Secure password hashing (bcrypt)
  • Regular security monitoring and audit logging

5. Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained until you delete your account
  • Conversation logs: Retained for up to 90 days on a rolling basis, then automatically purged
  • Security logs: Retained for 30 days
  • Post-deletion: All personal data erased within 30 days of account deletion, except where retention is required by law

6. Your Rights Under the DPDP Act

As a Data Principal under Indian law, you have the right to:

  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Withdraw consent: Withdraw your consent at any time — the process is as simple as providing consent
  • Grievance: File a complaint with us or with the Data Protection Board of India

To exercise any of these rights, contact our Grievance Officer (details below).
