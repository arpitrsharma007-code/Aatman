Delete Your Account

We respect your right to control your personal data

⚠ Please Read Before Proceeding

Deleting your Aatman account is permanent and irreversible. Once deleted, you will lose access to your account, all conversation history, and any active subscription benefits.

If you have an active paid subscription, please cancel it first to avoid being charged after deletion.

How to Delete Your Account

1

Open Aatman

Log in to your Aatman account on the web app

2

Go to Settings

Tap the menu icon and navigate to Account Settings

3

Select "Delete My Account"

Scroll to the bottom of the settings page and tap "Delete My Account"

4

Confirm Your Identity

Re-enter your password to verify your identity

5

Confirm Deletion

Review the data that will be deleted and confirm your decision

What Data Gets Deleted

Account information (name, email) Permanently deleted
Conversation history Permanently deleted
Subscription data Permanently deleted
Authentication credentials Permanently deleted
Security logs (IP, device info) Deleted within 30 days
Payment records Retained by Razorpay per their policy

Deletion Timeline

Immediately: Account access revoked, login disabled
Within 24 hours: Account and conversation data purged from primary database
Within 30 days: All remaining data (backups, security logs) permanently erased
Confirmation email sent to your registered email upon successful deletion

Alternative: Request Deletion via Email

If you are unable to delete your account through the app, you can request deletion by emailing us:

Email: grievance@aatman.app

Please include your registered email address in the request. We will verify your identity and process the deletion within 72 hours.

Your Rights

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, you have the right to request erasure of your personal data at any time. We are legally obligated to comply with such requests. If you are unsatisfied with how your deletion request was handled, you may file a complaint with the Data Protection Board of India.
